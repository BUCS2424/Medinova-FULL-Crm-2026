Durable Medical Equipment (DME) includes medical devices prescribed by doctors for home use, such as knee braces, back braces, wheelchairs, CPAP machines, and blood pressure monitors. Medicare Part B covers DME when medically necessary and prescribed by your healthcare provider.

Yes, Medicare Part B covers durable medical equipment when prescribed by your doctor and deemed medically necessary. As a Medicare-accredited supplier, MediNova handles all insurance paperwork for you, making the process simple and hassle-free.
